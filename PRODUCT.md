# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners and businesses in Spain looking to renovate or decorate spaces. Homeowners want their home transformed with confidence and without stress; businesses need a reliable partner for commercial fitouts. Both arrive with a specific project in mind and need to trust the company before reaching out. They compare multiple services and decide quickly based on first impressions of professionalism and portfolio quality.

## Product Purpose

Landing page for Decorreformas, a renovation and decoration studio based in Spain. The site exists to convert visitors into leads: someone lands on the page, sees the work and the people behind it, feels confident, and contacts the team. Success means a filled contact form or a phone call.

## Brand Personality

Professional, confiable, cercana (professional, reliable, close). The brand is a firm that knows what it is doing and communicates that without distance or formality. Clients feel they are dealing with experts who will listen to them. Not cold, not flashy.

## Anti-references

- Construction companies: orange/yellow palettes, hard-hat icons, rough industrial aesthetics.
- Generic startups: corporate blue, floating 3D illustration heroes, identical icon+heading+text card grids.
- Old-guard luxury: gold accents, ornamental serif fonts, marble textures, the look of an old-school real estate or law firm.

## Design Principles

1. **Trust before conversion.** Every section earns credibility before asking for anything. Show the work, show the people, show past clients. The CTA comes after trust is established, not before.
2. **Spaces speak.** The product is physical transformation. Photography of finished spaces carries more weight than copy. Design clears the way for images, not the other way around.
3. **Warmth without softness.** The brand is approachable but competent. Warm tones and human copy, never timid or apologetic layout choices.
4. **Spanish clarity.** Copy is direct and conversational Spanish, no corporate filler, no anglicisms. The client feels spoken to, not pitched at.
5. **One action per section.** Each section has a single purpose. No section tries to do two things at once.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Body text at 4.5:1 contrast or better. No color as the sole carrier of meaning. Reduced-motion alternative for all transitions. Language tag set to `es`.
