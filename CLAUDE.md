## Design Context

This project is **Decorreformas**, a Spanish renovation and decoration studio landing page. Register: **brand**. Full context is in [PRODUCT.md](PRODUCT.md) and [DESIGN.md](DESIGN.md).

**Key strategic principles:**
1. Trust before conversion — earn credibility before asking for anything.
2. Photography speaks — images carry the product; layout serves them.
3. Warmth without softness — approachable but competent; never timid.
4. Spanish clarity — direct conversational copy, no corporate filler.
5. One action per section — no section does two things.

**Design system snapshot:**
- Register: brand landing page (Spain market)
- Color strategy: Committed — deep teal (oklch ~0.34 hue 188°) on pure white `#ffffff`
- Accent: sage olive (oklch ~0.62 hue 140°) for small elements only
- Typography: single humanist sans (DM Sans / Plus Jakarta Sans preferred; Geist fallback), three weights
- Motion: choreographed scroll-driven with Framer Motion; `prefers-reduced-motion` required
- Anti-references: construction orange/yellow, corporate blue/startup generic, old-guard ornamental luxury, AI cream/sand backgrounds
